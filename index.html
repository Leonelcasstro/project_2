FREIGHT

Freight is a versatile and comprehensive type family available on Adobe Fonts. Designed by Joshua Darden, Freight is known for its exceptional readability and flexibility, making it suitable for a wide range of design projects, from editorial layouts to branding materials

Characteristics & Optical Sizing
Freight includes different optical sizes, ranging from text to display. This means that the typeface is optimized for various sizes of text, ensuring excellent legibility and readability across different contexts. The text styles are designed for smaller sizes, such as body text, while the display styles are tailored for larger sizes, such as headlines and titles.
The Freight type family is characterized by its balanced proportions, sturdy letterforms, and subtle detailing. These features contribute to its readability and make it a reliable choice for long-form content as well as shorter pieces of text.
